TDF Spaces

Design and innovation is the central focus here
ARCHITECTURE INTERIOR ARCHITECTURE INTERIOR DESIGN FACADES DESIGNS LANDSCAPE DESIGN REAL ESTATE CONSULATION SERVICES
Design and innovation is the central focus here. The embodiment of designing a space that fits the usage of the users. When we’re designing spaces for clients, we take into consideration their lifestyle above anything. We don’t focus on the aesthetic part of the space and then wrap the lifestyle around it. On the contrary, we reverse engineer the process by understanding the engine behind their characters (for residential spaces) and their brand identities/ cultures (for commercial spaces) that motorizes their lifestyles and work out the aesthetics accordingly.

TDF Boutique

Furniture, fixtures & accessories (FF&A) are the heart & soul of every space.
SELECTING SOURCING BUYING DESIGNING & PRODUCING
TDF boutique started out as an FF&A department in the design firm, with its scope ever-changing to fit the needs of the projects & the firm. We have now branched out as a platform/studio on its own. Our growing team are talented designers, creatives who share the same love for curation. We aim to upgrade people’s life through upgrading the aesthetics of their surroundings yet keeping it real & practical.
